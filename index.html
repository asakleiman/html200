Hey Look, Here's Some Words

Responsive web design is important because of the explosion of diversity of devices that pages are viewed with. No longer can a few common resolutions be handcrafted, as anything from a smartwatch to a 4k panel might be displaying the page to the user. Furthermore, accessibility, due to the fundamental necessity of the web to everyday life, has never been more imporant, and responsive principles allow readers and other methods of access to work smoothly for those that can't visually access pages - which also makes makes them more open to the bots that categorize and catalog the web.
